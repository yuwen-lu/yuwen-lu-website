Goalplay Video Coach

An AI-powered video coach APP for soccer goalkeepers.
In cooperation with Goalplay GmbH & Co. KG, part of the iOS practicum at Technical University of Munich ( iPraktikum 2019 ).

Real Industry Client Goalplay

Our project worked with the real client Goalplay, a company that combines the latest technology and the goalkeeper training founded by the legendary German goalkeeper Oliver Kahn.

Our team implemented industry-level Agile development principles and paid attention to the usability of the app.

We delivered our project through a Design Review and a Client Acceptance Test. Click on the links to watch the recordings of our presentations.

Machine Learning-Powered

Our app enabled goalkeepers to record their training videos and replay them, so that they can detect flaws in their practices from an objective view. We also offered personalized training plans for users. More than that, we integrated Machine Learning classification to detect the goalkeepers' performed training tasks (left/right dive, jump catch, etc.) and automatically calculate the completion status of their training plans. In the future, we can build other machine learning models to analyse the users' performance such as dive distance and jump height, to offer more professional feedback for their training.

As one developer in the team, I contributed to building the app using Swift and integrated the newly-released Apple ARKit motion detection module into our machine learning pipeline. I integrated the module to detect human's joint positions in the video frame and fed the data to our LSTM-based model, which conducted the classification.

A User-first Approach

As the Usability Manager of the team, I ensured our app meets the potential users' needs and implemented human-centered design principles.

Here are some activities we conducted as parts of our user-centered design:

  • User research (at a local football academy)
  • Defining user persona
  • Continuous usability evaluation using the provided framework CuuSE
  • Usability heuristics evaluation
  • User test
